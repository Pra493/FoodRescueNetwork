About Us

We are a community-driven initiative dedicated to rescuing surplus food and redistributing it to those in need.

How It Works

Our process is simple:

  • Restaurants and grocery stores donate surplus food.
  • Volunteers collect and distribute food to those in need.
  • Communities come together to reduce food waste and hunger.

Get Involved

  • Donate surplus food
  • Spread awareness
